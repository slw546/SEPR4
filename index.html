Bear Traffic Controller: Over the Cloud

Bear Traffic Controller: Over the Cloud is the new multiplayer Air Traffic Management game from team FLR.

Learn more

 

Network Multiplayer

Bear Traffic Controller: Over the Cloud features networked multiplayer allowing you to play an opponent on a different computer.

The aim of multiplayer is to attempt to capture your oponent's airspace by successfully navigating aircraft from their entry point to their exit point. The more aircraft you successfully navigate, the more of your openents airspace you will control.

Yet more bears

Team FLR has a history of brining you fun bear themed games, and this game is no different.

Bear Traffic Controller: Over the Cloud is the continuation of the original Bear Traffic Controller game and is brought to you by the original bear including team. The story continues as you compete against other players in order to prepare for the winter and provide for your family. But there is a twist, you may not be the only bear.

Non-network Single Player

Although Bear Traffic Controller: Over the Cloud is predominantly a multiplayer game, we haven't forgotten our single player gamers and single player is now better that ever

Single player includes two airport with land and take-off commands and a special ending that you will have to play to discover.
